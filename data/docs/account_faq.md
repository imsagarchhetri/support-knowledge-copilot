# Account FAQ

## Resetting your password
To reset your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot password". Enter
the email associated with your account. You will receive a reset link valid for
30 minutes. If the link expires, request a new one. Password resets are rate
limited to 5 attempts per hour.

## Changing your email address
Open Settings > Profile and update the email field. A confirmation email is sent
to the new address. The change takes effect only after you click the
confirmation link. Until then, the old email remains active.

## Two-factor authentication
We support TOTP authenticator apps and SMS codes. Enable 2FA under
Settings > Security. Back-up recovery codes are shown once at setup — store them
safely. If you lose access to your 2FA device, use a recovery code or contact
support with identity verification.

## Deleting your account
Account deletion is permanent and removes all data after a 14-day grace period.
Start deletion under Settings > Privacy. During the grace period you can cancel
deletion by logging back in.
