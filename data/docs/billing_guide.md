# Billing Guide

## Plans and pricing
We offer Free, Pro, and Enterprise plans. Pro is billed monthly or annually.
Annual billing includes a 20% discount. Enterprise pricing is custom and
includes SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated support contact.

## Upgrading and downgrading
Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remaining billing
period. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period so you
keep paid features until then.

## Refunds and error code BIL-402
Refunds are available within 14 days of a charge for annual plans. Monthly
charges are non-refundable but you can cancel to stop future charges. If you see
error code BIL-402 at checkout, the payment provider declined the card; update
the card under Settings > Billing and retry.

## Invoices and receipts
Invoices are generated on the billing date and emailed to the account owner.
Download past invoices under Settings > Billing > History. VAT details can be
added per workspace.
